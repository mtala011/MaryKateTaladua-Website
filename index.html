Mary Kate Taladua

Illustrator

My name is Mary Kate Taladua,a freelance illustrator and character concept artist. I am mostly known as CATHPALUGS across social media.

I'm an illustrator and character concept artist currently studying fine arts at UCR. I have worked for several artbooks for fan content of multiple sources of media, but primarily video games and Japanese anime. Notably are my illustrations for Demon and Love, Golden Dearest, Fire Emblem Three Houses Yearbook, Spectrum, and much more. I currently do not have any experience creating concept artwork professionally but I have been studying it for the past year.

I studied animation for a year under several directors' guidance and can do basic/idle character animations if needed. I have experience creating background artwork for animations as well.
